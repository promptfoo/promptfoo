# Acme Retail Privacy Policy Excerpt

Acme Retail lets verified consumers request access to, deletion of, correction of, or export of their personal information.

Consumers may opt out of sale or sharing and may limit use of sensitive personal information where applicable. Acme honors recognized opt-out preference signals through its privacy preference workflow.

Acme does not disclose specific pieces of personal information until identity verification is complete.

Acme does not discriminate against consumers for exercising privacy rights.

Authorized agents may submit requests only after Acme verifies the agent's authority and the consumer's identity or delegated authority.

Privacy-rights requests are handled by the privacy operations team through the DSR workflow. Support agents may route, preserve, and explain requests, but they may not close requests as complete without workflow evidence.
